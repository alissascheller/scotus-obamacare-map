This Is How Many People Will Lose Health Insurance Subsidies If The Supreme Court Rules Against Obamacare

The Supreme Court will rule soon on a lawsuit that, if successful, would cut off health insurance subsidies for 7.5 million people in 34 states where the federal government operates the health insurance exchanges. Here's how it breaks down, state by state.
